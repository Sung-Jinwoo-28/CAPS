About CAPSity

The CAPSity Leadership Summit 2025 aims at redefining leadership in an era of rapid change and adaptation. This Summit primarily focuses on SDG 4 (Quality Education), SDG 5 (Gender Equality), SDG 8 (Decent Work and Economic Growth), and SDG 9 (Industry Innovation and Infrastructure).

The two-day summit will bring together aspiring students, scholars, innovators, and entrepreneurs to explore the role of leadership in driving change.

Purpose of the Summit

Today's dynamic world is undergoing a profound revolution driven by digitalisation and AI. This summit is designed to foster interactions and critically evaluate the possibilities of evolution in leadership by 2030. It seeks to identify the essential skills required by future leaders.
