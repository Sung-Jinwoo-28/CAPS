About CAPS Confluence

The CAPS Confluence 2026 is a summit designed to bring students face-to-face with ideas, journeys, and conversations that shape real-world scenarios. Through immersive formats such as the Seminar, Panel Discussion, Certified Workshops and Fireside Chat, the event channels honest dialogue, interdisciplinary exchange and practical skill-development.

Participants engage with Industry Leaders and Change Makers who share not just success stories but the essence of challenges, failures and turning points that redefined their paths. By blending inspiration with application, the event bridges the gap between academic learning and professional development. It encourages students to explore evolving career landscapes shaped by innovation, technology, design, and human-centred thinking through critical thinking.

Purpose of the Confluence

In today’s rapidly evolving world shaped by Digitalisation and Artificial Intelligence, the role of leadership has been fundamentally redefined. This event aims to spark critical dialogue on how leaders must adapt, evolve and build resilience to navigate change and shape the future. It focuses on identifying essential future-ready skills and exploring how learning ecosystems can transform individuals to prepare and lead meaningfully in an ever-changing professional ecosystem.
